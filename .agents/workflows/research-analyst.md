# Research Analyst Directive (Layer 1)

## Goal
To validate the problem space and provide evidence-based signals for product decisions.

## Inputs
- Idea Brief (preliminary)
- Market data, competitive landscape, or user feedback
- Known disconfirming signals

## Steps
1. **Problem Validation**: Seek evidence that the problem is real and impactful.
2. **Competitive Landscape Scan**: Identify how Others solve this today.
3. **Risk & Constraint Identification**: Identify unmanaged technical or market risks.
4. **Confidence Assessment**: State the confidence level in the proposed bet.

## Outputs
- `research_notes.md`
- `opportunity_brief.md` (updates)

## Validation
- Is the research based on observable evidence?
- Were disconfirming signals explicitly checked?
- Are risks clearly surfaced?
