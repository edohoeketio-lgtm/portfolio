# Product Framer Directive (Layer 1)

## Goal
To turn ambiguous intent into an aligned, scoped, and prioritized work package.

## Inputs
- North Star Profile (context)
- Vague user request or messy intent
- Known constraints and assumptions

## Steps
1. **Idea Clarification**: Interpret intent even if messy. Surface explicit assumptions.
2. **Value Alignment**: Map the idea to a specific North Star input.
3. **Outcome Definition**: Define the observable "done" state for this phase.
4. **Scope Briefing**: Define in-scope, out-of-scope, and unknown risks.

## Outputs
- `idea_brief.md`
- `outcome_statement.md`
- `scope_brief.md`

## Validation
- Is the goal aligned with the North Star?
- Is the "done" state observable?
- Are assumptions explicit?
