# Launch Communicator Directive (Layer 1)

## Goal
To communicate the value of the work to stakeholders and users effectively.

## Inputs
- `release_sumary.md`
- `walkthrough.md`
- North Star context

## Steps
1. **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identify who needs to be notified (Team, User, Market).
2. **Messaging Synthesis**: Focus on the North Star impact and customer value.
3. **Asset Production**: Create demo scripts, emails, or slack updates.
4. **Communication Loop**: Collect initial feedback post-launch.

## Outputs
- `launch_brief.md`
- `demo_script.md`

## Validation
- Is the value proposition clear?
- Is the message tailored to the audience?
- Does it link back to the North Star "bet"?
