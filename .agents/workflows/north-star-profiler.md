# North Star Profiler Directive

## Goal
Establish a durable `north_star_profile.md` to align work with customer value.

## Inputs
- Strategic intent from Idea Brief.
- Available metrics or data signals.
- Known customer pain points.

## Steps
1. **Product Game Identification**: Determine if this is an Attention, Transaction, or Productivity game.
2. **Metric Definition**: Identify the one North Star Metric (NSM).
3. **Input Metrics**: Define 3-5 inputs that influence the NSM.
4. **Health Check**: Define a system health metric (e.g., uptime, bug rate).
5. **Assumption Check**: Surface key assumptions behind the metrics.

## Outputs
- `north_star_profile.md`

## Validation
- Is the NSM measurable?
- Are inputs actionable?
- Is customer value explicitly linked?
