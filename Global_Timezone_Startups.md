# The Global Scale List: LATAM & APAC (Remote WAT Alignment)

Applying from Lagos (WAT) puts you in a strategic time zone pocket. 

**LATAM (UTC-3 to UTC-5):** You overlap with their afternoons entirely while having deeply focused, async mornings. LATAM FinTech is currently exploding, heavily funded by Y Combinator, and deeply technical.
**APAC / India (UTC+5:30 to UTC+8):** You overlap heavily with their mornings and early afternoons. Peak XV (formerly Sequoia India) has turned the region into an absolute powerhouse for DevTools, APIs, and B2B SaaS architecture.

Here are 20 highly capitalized, engineering-heavy startups in these regions where your Web3/Aave ledger background and rigorous frontend architecture (like your Playwright/PostHog integration) will make you a top-tier candidate.

## Part 1: LATAM (Y Combinator & High-Growth FinTech)
LATAM startups are obsessed with building API infrastructure and payment gateways to unify a highly fragmented continent. Building these merchant dashboards requires intense frontend data-fetching and robust state management.

### The Startups
1.  **[Belvo](https://belvo.com)** (Open Banking API) - Essentially the "Plaid" of Latin America. Building developer portals and consent dashboards here requires absolute state security.
2.  **[Clara](https://clara.com)** (Corporate Spend Management) - Unicorn status. Building complex expense management interfaces where UI latency is unacceptable.
3.  **[Yuno](https://yuno.com)** (Payment Orchestration) - Massive recent funding to unify payment methods across LatAm. High-stakes merchant dashboards.
4.  **[Pomelo](https://pomelo.la)** (Cards & Payments Infrastructure) - They provide the backend for fintechs. They need senior engineers to build their complex B2B client configuration portals.
5.  **[Truora](https://truora.com)** (Authentication & WhatsApp Integration) - Fast-growing YC company focused on user identity verification. 
6.  **[Nowports](https://nowports.com)** (Digital Freight Forwarder) - Similar to MyDello but mapping the insane logistics of Latin America. Huge data-density required for tracking UIs.
7.  **[Fintual](https://fintual.com)** (Wealth Management) - YC-backed. Building highly performant investment dashboards for the consumer market.
8.  **[Kushki](https://kushkipagos.com)** (Payment Infrastructure) - Another massive unicorn in the payment gateway space. Huge engineering operation solving high-volume transaction UX.
9.  **[Jeeves](https://tryjeeves.com)** (Global/LATAM Spend Management) - Heavy focus on global companies. Managing multi-currency expenditure UI.
10. **[Buk](https://buk.cl)** (HR Tech Platform) - Huge SaaS platform managing payroll and HR. Requires highly rigorous, error-proof form handling and data rendering.

## Part 2: APAC / India (Peak XV & Elite DevTools)
The startups coming out of India right now are heavily skewed towards global B2B SaaS, developer tooling, and massive-scale data collaboration. They value hardcore computer science fundamentals and deeply interactive UIs.

### The Startups
11. **[Atlan](https://atlan.com)** (Data Collaboration) - Active metadata platform for data teams. Building a collaborative data workspace is an insane React state management challenge.
12. **[Appsmith](https://appsmith.com)** (Open Source Internal Tools) - Building a platform for other developers to build UI. You literally have to be a master of React architecture to work here.
13. **[Hasura](https://hasura.io)** (GraphQL APIs) - Core infrastructure company. Their web console is a highly complex playground for querying databases directly.
14. **[Smallcase](https://smallcase.com)** (Investment Products) - Peak XV backed. Building modern stock market investment UIs for the massive Indian retail market.
15. **[Chargebee](https://chargebee.com)** (Subscription Billing) - Massive global unicorn. Handling complex subscription lifecycles, churn interfaces, and enterprise billing dashboards.
16. **[BrowserStack](https://browserstack.com)** (Web/App Testing) - A pioneer in browser infrastructure. You love Playwright and E2E testing—this is the mothership of testing culture.
17. **[Signzy](https://signzy.com)** (AI Digital Onboarding) - Highly complex verification flows using AI. Frontends require extreme reliability and accessibility.
18. **[InVideo](https://invideo.io)** (AI Video Creation) - Building a full-blown video editor inside the browser using modern web APIs and Canvas/WebGL. Intensely technical frontend.
19. **[LambdaTest](https://lambdatest.com)** (Cloud Testing Platform) - Competitor to BrowserStack, massive growth, heavily invested in developer experience (DX).
20. **[Razorpay](https://razorpay.com)** (Payment Gateway) - The absolute giant of Indian FinTech. Building developer checkout experiences that must load in milliseconds with zero errors.
