# The Northern Tech Hub: Canada (Remote-Friendly)

Canada is experiencing an absolute boom in B2B SaaS, AI infrastructure, and DevTools. Companies in Toronto and Vancouver are heavily funded, deeply technical, and increasingly operating as fully remote or remote-first organizations. 

Because Canada is inextricably linked to the US tech ecosystem, these companies pay exceptionally well (easily clearing your $30,000 baseline, often pushing $60k-$100k+ USD for remote seniors) and expect Silicon Valley-level engineering rigor. 

**Timezone Note:** Canada spans from EST (Toronto/Montreal) to PST (Vancouver). EST is only 5-6 hours behind Lagos, giving you the same excellent LATAM-style overlap (your focus time in the morning, synchronous overlap in your afternoon).

Here are 10 of the fastest-growing, heavily capitalized Canadian SaaS startups that are actively hiring and building complex frontends.

### 🍁 The "Relocation & Visa Sponsorship" Track (Global Talent Stream)
Canada has a fast-track immigration program called the **Global Talent Stream (GTS)**, which processes visas for Software Engineers in just 2-4 weeks. Startups leverage this heavily to pull senior talent worldwide. If you are open to moving in 2025/2026, target these heavily funded companies known for comprehensive relocation packages (flights, temporary housing, and visa sponsorship):

11. **[Wealthsimple](https://www.wealthsimple.com/)** (Toronto) - The "Robinhood of Canada." Massive engineering team, famously generous with compensation and international relocation.
12. **[Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/)** (Toronto/Remote) - The giant. Highly remote, but they readily sponsor visas and relocate top-tier engineering talent to their Canadian hubs.
13. **[Klue](https://klue.com/)** (Vancouver) - Competitive enablement platform. They are growing rapidly and are known for bringing in international tech talent to Vancouver.
14. **[Shakepay](https://shakepay.com/)** (Montreal) - A massive crypto/bitcoin platform in Canada. Given your Web3/Aave background, this is an incredible fit. They actively hire and relocate seniors.
15. **[ApplyBoard](https://www.applyboard.com/)** (Kitchener/Waterloo) - EdTech unicorn built entirely by immigrants. They have massive dedicated teams for international relocation and visa sponsorship.

*Tip for Applications:* When asked "Do you require visa sponsorship?" on these applications, proudly check **YES**. They use the Global Talent Stream specifically for this purpose.
